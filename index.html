About TherapyPal

TherapyPal is an innovative AI-powered mental health platform designed to provide immediate, confidential support whenever you need it. Our advanced conversational AI has been trained by mental health professionals to offer compassionate, evidence-based guidance.

Whether you're dealing with stress, anxiety, depression, or just need someone to talk to, TherapyPal is available 24/7 to listen without judgment and help you develop coping strategies for better mental wellbeing.
